        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Preheat oven to 425Â°F. Combine butter, parsley, seasoned salt and garlic powder in a small bowl. Set aside.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 2:</strong>Ensure baked potatoes are cooled. Cut potatoes in half lengthwise. Using a small spoon, scoop out the flesh leaving a 1/4" shell
        <br><strong>Step 3:</strong>
        Brush both the inside and outside of the potatoes with the butter mixture. Place potatoes cut side down in baking dish. Bake 15 minutes.
        <br><strong>Step 4:</strong>

        Flip potatoes over and bake an additional 5 minutes or until slightly browned and crisp.
<br><strong>Step 5:</strong>
Fill each potato with cheese and bacon. Return to the oven for another 5-7 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
      <br><strong>Step 6:</strong>  
      Remove from the oven, top with chives and serve with sour cream.

        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Melt butter over medium heat in a Dutch oven or large saucepan
        <br>
        <strong>Step 2:</strong>Add onion wedges, water, can of tomatoes with their juices, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. 
        <br><strong>Step 3:</strong>
        Bring to a simmer. Cook, uncovered, for about 40 minutes. Stir occasionally and add additional salt as needed.
        <br><strong>Step 4:</strong>
Blend the soup, and then season to taste.

        <br><strong>Step 3:</strong>

 <br><strong>Step 4:</strong>

 <br><strong>Step 5:</strong>
Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter and slice of cheese.

        <table>

                <tr>
                    <th>Ingredients</th>

<td>Oreos</td>
               <td>14 oz.</td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                <td>Cream Cheese</td>
                <td> 8 oz.</td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                <td>Vanilla Extract</td>
                <td> 1 tsp.</td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                <td>White Chocolate Chips(Melted)</td>
                <td> 2 Cups</td>
                 </tr>
                 <tr>
                <td>Semisweet Chocolate Chips (Melted)</td>

            </tbody>
        </table>
        
        <p><strong>Step 1:</strong> Use a food processor to crush cookies into fine crumbs.
        <br>
        <strong>Step 2:</strong>Add all but 2 tablespoons crushed cookies to a medium bowl. Add cream cheese and vanilla and stir until evenly combined.
        <br><strong>Step 3:</strong>
 Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Using a small cookie scoop, form mixture into small balls. Place on prepared baking sheet and freeze until slightly hardened, about 30 minutes.      
 <br><strong>Step 4:</strong>
 Dip the frozen balls in melted white chocolate until coated and return to baking sheet. Drizzle with semisweet chocolate. Freeze until chocolate hardens, about 15 minutes.
        </p>
